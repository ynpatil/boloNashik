Email Templates Home

View existing email templates on this page. This page displays the following information.

  • A Search sub-panel where you can enter the template name and description to search for a specific template.
  • A list of existing email templates along with related information such as names and description.
    • To view a template, click its name.
    • To export one or more templates, select them from the list, click Export, and choose Selected Records. To export all the templates listed on the page or all existing templates, select Current Page and Entire List respectively.
    • To delete one or more templates, select them from the list and click Delete.
