Emails

Use the Emails module to manage your inbound and outbound emails.

The Emails Home page displays the following information:

  • A Search sub-panel where you can enter information such as the email status and subject to find a specific case.
  • A list of emails in your inbox along with related information such as the subject and contacts.
    • To open an email that you received, click its subject in My Inbox sub-panel.
    • To assign yourself an email from the Group inbox, click Take From Group.
    • To update, delete, or archive multiple emails, select them from the list and use the Mass Update sub-panel.
    • To export one or more emails, select them from the list, click Export and choose Selected Records, Current Page, or Entire List as appropriate.
  • A Shortcuts section that displays the following options:
    • My Inbox. Click this option to view the list of emails in your inbox.
    • My Drafts. Click this option to view emails that you saved as drafts.
    • Sent Emails. Click this option to view a list of emails that you sent out.
    • My Archives. Click this option to view a list of emails that you archived for your records.
    • Group Inbox. Click this option to view inbound emails that the system routed to the Group inbox.
    • Compose Email. Click this option to create an email.
    • Create Archived Email. Click this option to archive an inbound or outbound email for your records.
    • Create Email Template. Click this option to create a template for campaign emails. The system extracts variables such as names and addresses from campaign target records and merges them with the email template when you send out campaign emails.
    • All Drafts. Click this option to view all draft emails.
    • All Emails. Click this option to view all outbound emails including drafts and archived emails.
    • Email Templates. Click this option to view a list of existing email templates.
