View Emails

Use this page to view your emails that you saved as drafts, or emails that you sent, or emails that you archived.

This page displays the following information:

  • A Search panel where you can enter information such as the subject or contact name to search for emails. Enter the date and time when the email was sent.
  • A list of emails that you sent, drafted, or archived; click an email subject to view the contents of the email.
  • A Mass Update panel that you can use to update or delete multiple emails selected from the list.
