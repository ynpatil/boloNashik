Notes Home

Use the Notes Home page to create and manage notes related to an activity.

The Notes Home page displays the following information:

  • A Search sub-panel where you can enter the subject or a contact name to search for a specific note. To perform an advanced search using additional fields, click the Advanced Search tab. To customize and save the search layout and results, click the Saved Search & Layout tab.
  • A list of existing notes along with related information such as contact and any attachment.
  • To view the note details, click its subject.
  • To update or delete multiple notes, select them from the list, and use the Mass Update sub-panel to perform the action.
