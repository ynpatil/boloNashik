Outbound Email Management

Use this page to manage mass campaign emails that are in the queue for dispatch.

This page displays the following information:

  • A Search sub-panel where you can enter the campaign name, the recipient's name and email address to search for a specific campaign.
  • A Shortcuts menu that displays the following option:
    • View Queued Emails. Click this option to navigate back to the Outbound Email Queue Managment page.
  • A list of existing email queues.
To delete one or more emails in the queue, select them from the list and click Delete.
